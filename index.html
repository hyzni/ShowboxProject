About

SHOWBOX
CORP.,

A part of the Orion Group, is a Korean content studio which has been at the forefront of the Korean film industry for over 20 years and is known for its outstanding performance in the local Box Office and its active participation in the international marketplace.

Established in 2002, SHOWBOX has built itself to encompass the entire production, financing, and distribution sectors, providing the filmmakers a solid production ground to create entertaining content for the audience, with 'entertainment' and 'quality' as its foremost principles.

SHOWBOX has created

line

substantial record-breaking hits over the years, such as TAEGUKGI(2004), THE HOST(2005), THE THIEVES(2012), ASSASSINATION(2015), A TAXI DRIVER(2017) and EXHUMA(2024), each crossing 10 million admissions. Moreover, SHOWBOX boasts a large library of films, with some of its biggest titles being: WELCOME TO DONGMAKGOL(2005), NAMELESS GANGSTER: RULES OF THE TIME(2012), THE FACE READER(2013), A HARD DAY(2014), THE THRONE(2015), THE CLASSIFIED FILE(2015), INSIDE MEN(2015), A VIOLENT PROSECUTOR(2016), TUNNEL(2016), MEMOIR OF A MURDERER(2017), GONJIAM: HAUNTED ASYLUM(2018), THE MAN STANDING NEXT(2020), SINKHOLE(2021) and EMERGENCY DECLARATION(2022). Based on ample experience in the distribution sector, SHOWBOX has successfully made its mark in both TV-series production, as proved by the global hit ITAEWON CLASS(2020) and A KILLER PARADOX(2024), and international co-production, as demonstrated by the Korea-Thai project THE MEDIUM(2021). SHOWBOX continues to maximize opportunities by establishing strategic partnerships in the web-comics business and securing original IPs in order to develop various formats of creative content targeting global markets. SHOWBOX leads the industry at the center of K-content, based on its rich experience in creating content with talented professionals and deep insights into the content ecosystem.
